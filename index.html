Disappearance of Alice

Unearth the mystery

It's been thirteen years since the disappearance of Alice. An annual blanket of mist engulfs the city on the day of her disappearance. What's truly unsettling is the fact that no single citizen in Inaba was able to recount what happened on the day of Alice's disappearance. It's almost as if an ominious otherworldly evil vanquished the memories of anybody who ever came in contact with her. As a long standing cold case, it's now up to you to unearth the mystery.
